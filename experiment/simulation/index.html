STEP 1 : Make all the Correct Connections.

1.    S1 of MCB - A1 of Ammeter && A2 of Ammeter - V1 of Voltmeter

2.    V1 of Voltmeter - Mw of Wattmeter && L of Wattmeter - C of Wattmeter

3.    S2 of MCB - V of Wattmeter && V of Wattmeter - N1 of Energy Meter

4.    N2 of Energy Meter - L2 of Load && Le of Energy Meter - L1 of Load

5.    L of Wattmeter - M of Energy Meter && S2 of MCB - V2 of Voltmeter

STEP 2 : Click on "Check" Button.

STEP 3 : Click on the MCB to Turn ON the circuit.

STEP 4 : Select the number of Bulbs from the Lamp Load.

STEP 5 : Count the number of times Green LED blinks in 1min(Meter Counter) by using "STOPWATCH".

STEP 6 : Enter the number of times Green LED blinks in 1min(Meter Counter).

STEP 7 : Note down the readings of the Ammeter, Voltmeter & Wattmeter.

STEP 8 : Click "ADD" button to add the values to the Observation Table.

STEP 9 : Add different values to the Table by selecting different number of bulbs.

STEP 10 : Click on "PRINT" button to print the page.

FORMULA

Actual Energy (A) = VIT/1000 kw-hr

Recorded Energy (R) = Number of Impulse(1min)/Meter Constant

Meter Constant = 3200 Imp/kw-hr

Relative Error = ((R-A)/A)*100%
