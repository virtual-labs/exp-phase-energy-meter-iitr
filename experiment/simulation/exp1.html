1. Click on EQUIPMENT SETUP Button.

2. After this, you need to setup autolevel instrument by placing it on the tripod using keyboard keys i.e.l->left movement,r->right movement,u->up movement,d->down movement.

3. After this, click on LEVEL SETUP Button & setup blue bubble dot to the center by using keyboard keys i.e.a->left movement,b->right movement,f->up movement,g->down movement..

4.After this, click on the NEXT Button.

5. Click on the START Button, staff at point A will appear.

6. Click on the staff at point A & after this, click on the SAVE Button to save readings at point A.After this click on NEXT Button to move the staff at point B.

7. Repeat step 6 for points B,C & D.

8. After click on VIEW READINGS Button to view saved readings.

9. Alidade view of each point is also given at the left side of the pane.

10. Click on the FINISH Button to finish the simulation.
